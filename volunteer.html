Volunteer

What:
Volunteers help 3 shift captains on cooking shifts prepare food for our clients. Volunteers can help with any task in the kitchen including deciding what to cook, preparing meat, and chopping vegetables. Cooking shifts are scheduled for two hours, but generally last from an hour to an hour and a half.

When:
We have cooking shifts at the following times:

  • Monday 6:00-8:00 PM
  • Tuesday 6:00-8:00 PM
  • Thursday 6:00-8:00 PM
  • Saturday 11:30 AM-1:30 PM
Where:
Volunteers for all cooking shifts will meet in the kitchen in the First Congregational Church, across the gate next to Hitzeman mailroom.

How to sign up:
Use this link to pick the date you would like to come volunteer with us! We are excited to work with you!
