Our Events

OUR EVENTS SPREAD AWARENESS AND PROMOTE EXCITEMENT FOR OUR MISSION.

We regularly hold especially publicized events to invite as many members of the WashU community to participate in and support our efforts to feed St. Louis. Look out for our advertising to join in the festivities!

PIE CARAMBA

At Pie Caramba, we invite volunteers to come make two pies: one to donate to our clients, and one for them to bring home to eat. Volunteers can sign up in groups up to 4 people. This event occurs late in the fall semester.

PIZZA PALOOZA

At Pizza Palooza, volunteers come in groups of up to 4 people to make two pizzas: one to donate to our clients, and one for them to eat. This event occurs late in the spring semester.

RAISE THE DOUGH

For Raise the Dough, Shift Captains sell donuts to raise money for our team. The donuts' icings are homemade by Shift Captains. Our flavors have included Oreo, Matcha, Coffee, Strawberry, and Nutella.
