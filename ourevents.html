Our Events

Pie Caramba

At Pie Caramba, we invite volunteers to come make two pies: one to donate to our clients, and one for them to bring home to eat. Volunteers can sign up in groups up to 4 people. This event occurs late in the fall semester.

Pizza Palooza

At Pizza Palooza, volunteers come in groups of up to 4 people to make two pizzas: one to donate to our clients, and one for them to eat. This event occurs late in the spring semester.
