Fighting waste, feeding people.

THE CAMPUS KITCHEN X FOOD RECOVERY NETWORK AT WASHINGTON UNIVERSITY IN ST. LOUIS FIGHTS TO SIMULTANEOUSLY ELIMINATE FOOD INSECURITY AND FOOD WASTE IN OUR COMMUNITY.

St. Louis City is one of the most food insecure cities in the nation, with a food insecurity rate of 23.3%. Nationwide, 40% of food is wasted, going straight to landfill. At CKxFRN WashU, we safely recover foods and ingredients, use them to prepare meals, and deliver them to our clients, who serve the most food insecure groups in St. Louis. Together, we can divert food waste and fight hunger in our own community.
